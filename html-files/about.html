Hi, I'm Amelia K Bujar

My origin story started in Koszalin, Poland on the day of 25th December, 2002 before moving to Bergen, Norway when i was 5 years old.

I'm a front-end development, and in my free time also a very enthusiastic book reader which you can probably tell from this website

What's The Book Corner Chroincles?

The Book Corner Chroincles is a website where i review books, poems, short stoires and comic books

The Book Corner Chroincles was started on July 13th, 2020 and since that time i reviewed a lot of books, interviewed a fair share of not widely known authors and i have done requested reviews of books where the publisher or the author themselves have send me their books to review.

What's The motivation behind The Book Corner Chroincles?

the motivation behind The Book Corner Chroincles, is to share my opinions about books, comic books, poems and short stories I've read but at the same time help new authors and not well known authors to get their books exposed to a bigger audience through this website

Is The Book Corner Chroincles on other platforms ?

Yes, you can find The Book Corner Chroincles on instagram and you can find The Book Corner Chroincles on YouTube where you can find all of the video reviews I've done with authors

The Book corner Chroincles book club

this website a official book club on discord where anyone can join to talk about books, read and publish recommendations, seek book recommendations and to read alongs with other members of the server
